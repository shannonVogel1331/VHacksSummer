Troubleshooting Guide: Failed Prints

If you are using Ultimaker Cura, before following the steps below go to:

Preferences > Configure Cura > Settings > Check All

To turn on visibility for all settings.

  • Solutions when print is not sticking to bed:
    • Clean build plate
    • Printer may not be leveled closely enough, re-level the build plate.
  • Solutions when not extruding at start of print or mid-print:
    • Grinding filament:
      • Clean out extruder box.
      • Lessen tension on filament, usually a screw you can adjust: check documentation for your specific model.
    • Clogged extruder:
      • Heat extruder and de-clog with thin wire often found in maintenance kit.
      • Perform a hot pull cold pull. Ultimaker has a good guide for this procedure.
  • Solutions when supports are knocked over:
    • Make sure the printer is leveled evenly.
    • Add adhesion (raft works best).
    • Decrease the build plate temperature.
