Troubleshooting Guide: Low Quality Prints

If you are using Ultimaker Cura, before following the steps below go to:

Preferences > Configure Cura > Settings > Check All

To turn on visibility for all settings.

  • Solutions for Under-extrusion:
    • Check filament diameters: materials properties.
      • In Ultimaker Cura: preferences > configure Cura > materials > diameter
    • Increase extrusion multiplier.
      • In Ultimaker Cura: settings > flow rate compensation factor
  • Solutions for Over-extrusion:
    • Check filament diameters: materials properties.
      • In Ultimaker Cura: preferences > configure Cura > materials > diameter
    • Decrease extrusion multiplier.
      • In Ultimaker Cura: settings > flow rate compensation factor
  • Solutions to Stringing/Oozing
    • Retraction Distance
      • Increase retraction distance by 1 mm at a time
      • In Ultimaker Cura: settings > travel > retraction distance
    • Retraction Speed
      • Increase retraction speed
      • Ideal range is 1200-6000 mm/min or 20-100 mm/s
      • Settings > travel > retraction speed
    • Temperature
      • Decrease temperature by 5-10 degrees
      • Settings > materials > printing temperature
    • Long movements over open spaces can cause stringing/oozing
      • Best way to fix this is to change your design: your software should optimize when slicing for the shortest path possible.
    • Movement speed
      • If your printer can handle it, try increasing the speed
      • Tune while print is running and adjust speed
      • Settings > Speed > Print Speed
  • Solutions to Overheating
    • Insufficient Cooling
      • Increase fan power
      • Settings > Cooling >Fan Speed
    • Temperature
      • Decrease temperature by 5-10 degrees
      • Settings > materials > printing temperature
    • Movement Speed
      • Decrease the speed
      • Tune while print is running and adjust speed
      • Settings > Speed > Print Speed
    • Printing multiple parts at once allows for more time to cool
